## 文章看点

PromptFolder transforms prompt engineering from fragmented notes into a structured process by providing a centralized environment to draft, test, and organize LLM instructions. It boosts efficiency for power users through variable-based prompt construction and a personal library system, eliminating manual copy-pasting and enabling rapid iterative optimization.

## 内容预览

PromptFolder is a specialized utility designed for users who want to move beyond simple chat interfaces and treat prompt engineering as a structured process.

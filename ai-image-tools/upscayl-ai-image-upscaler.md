## 文章看点

Upscayl is a free, open-source AI image upscaler that transforms low-resolution photos into high-definition visuals with sharp edges and clean textures. By processing images locally on your desktop, it ensures maximum privacy and supports batch processing for an efficient, professional-grade workflow across Windows, macOS, and Linux.

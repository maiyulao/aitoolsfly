## 文章看点

Pebblely transforms simple product photos into professional studio-quality images by automating background removal and AI-driven scene generation. It enables e-commerce sellers to instantly create high-converting visuals with realistic lighting and diverse environments, eliminating the need for expensive photography equipment.

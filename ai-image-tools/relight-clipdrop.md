## 文章看点

Relight by ClipDrop lets you realistically adjust image lighting by placing virtual, color-customizable light sources in a 3D-like space. It leverages depth awareness to automate complex shading and highlight effects, providing photographers and artists an effortless way to achieve cinematic results without manual editing.

## 文章看点

Huiwa AI 3D transforms flat-lay clothing images and sketches into realistic 3D visualizations, eliminating the need for expensive photoshoots or complex modeling software. It enables fashion designers and e-commerce brands to rapidly prototype garments and create high-quality, volumetric product assets for instant visual impact.

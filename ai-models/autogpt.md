## 文章看点

AutoGPT is an autonomous AI agent that achieves high-level goals independently by automating the cycle of thinking, planning, and executing. It eliminates the need for constant prompting by decomposing complex tasks, browsing the web, and managing memory to deliver fully automated workflows.

## 内容预览

AutoGPT represents a significant leap in the evolution of Large Language Models by introducing the concept of an autonomous AI agent.

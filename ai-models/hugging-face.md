## 文章看点

The "GitHub of Machine Learning," Hugging Face provides a massive open-source ecosystem to host, collaborate on, and deploy state-of-the-art AI models and datasets. It enables developers and researchers to instantly integrate pre-trained models and launch interactive demos without training from scratch, drastically accelerating the AI development cycle.

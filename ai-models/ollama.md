## 文章看点

Ollama simplifies running powerful large language models like Llama 3 and Mistral locally on your own hardware, ensuring total data privacy and offline access. It removes complex configuration hurdles with an easy-to-use interface and automatic GPU acceleration, allowing developers to integrate local AI capabilities for free without costly cloud subscriptions.

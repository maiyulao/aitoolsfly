## 内容预览

OverviewGenerative AI for Beginners is a free, open-source curriculum developed by Microsoft to democratize AI education. Hosted on GitHub, this course provides a structured pathway for individuals with little to no prior experience in artificial intelligence to understand how Large Language Models (LLMs) work and how to leverage them in real-world scenarios.Key CapabilitiesFoundational Knowledge: Learn the core concepts of generative AI, including the architecture of transformers and how models are trained.Prompt Engineering: Master the art of crafting effective prompts to get high-quality, accurate outputs from AI models.Application Development: Move beyond simple chatting to understand how to integrate AI into software and build functional AI-powered apps.Hands-on Learning: Access practical exercises and code samples that allow students to apply theoretical knowledge immediately.Best ForStudents and professionals looking for a reputable, structured entry point into AI.Developers who want to understand the underlying logic of LLMs before building applications.Non-technical users interested in the practical utility and ethical considerations of generative AI.Limitations and PricingThe course content is entirely free and open-source. However, users should be aware that while the learning materials are free, accessing certain cloud platforms or API endpoints (such as Azure OpenAI Service) to practice the exercises may incur separate costs depending on the provider's pricing tiers.Disclaimer: Course content, availability, and third-party tool pricing are subject to change.

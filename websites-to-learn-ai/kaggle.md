## 文章看点

Kaggle is a premier global ecosystem for data science and machine learning, offering free GPU/TPU acceleration and a vast library of open-source datasets. Users can immediately level up their skills through interactive courses and high-stakes competitions to build a professional portfolio without needing expensive hardware.

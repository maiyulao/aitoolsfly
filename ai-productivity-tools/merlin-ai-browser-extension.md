## 文章看点

Merlin AI is a powerful browser extension that embeds AI directly into your workflow to summarize articles, draft emails, and enhance search results without switching tabs. It boosts productivity for professionals by automating content synthesis and generation across any webpage for instant efficiency.

Merlin AI is a sophisticated browser extension designed to bridge the gap between your web browsing experience and the power of large language models.

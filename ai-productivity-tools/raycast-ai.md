## 文章看点

Raycast AI transforms your Mac launcher into a powerful productivity hub, allowing you to draft text, generate code, and retrieve instant knowledge via keyboard shortcuts. It eliminates tedious context switching between apps, enabling macOS power users to maintain deep flow and automate repetitive tasks effortlessly.

## 内容预览

OverviewRaycast AI transforms the traditional Mac launcher into a comprehensive productivity hub by integrating advanced artificial intelligence directly into the command bar.

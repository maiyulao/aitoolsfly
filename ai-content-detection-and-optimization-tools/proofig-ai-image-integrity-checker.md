## 文章看点

Proofig is an AI-driven forensic tool that ensures scientific integrity by automatically detecting image manipulation, duplication, and plagiarism in research figures. It empowers journals and researchers to instantly spot fraudulent data, drastically streamlining the peer-review process and preventing academic misconduct.

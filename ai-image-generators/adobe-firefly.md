## 文章看点

Adobe Firefly is a professional generative AI engine designed for commercially safe image creation and seamless editing within the Adobe Creative Cloud ecosystem. It empowers designers to instantly generate high-resolution visuals and use AI-driven tools like Generative Fill to accelerate professional workflows with legal certainty.

OverviewAdobe Firefly is Adobe's state-of-the-art generative AI engine, built specifically for creative professionals.

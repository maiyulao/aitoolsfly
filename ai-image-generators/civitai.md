## 文章看点

Civitai is the central ecosystem for open-source AI art, offering a massive library of community-trained models and LoRAs to achieve precise artistic styles. It empowers users to instantly elevate their imagery through prompt sharing and browser-based generation, eliminating the need for high-end local hardware.

## 内容预览

Kai Pai, developed by Meitu, is a specialized AI video generation tool focused on creating high-quality "talking head" or spokesperson videos. It eliminates the need for expensive camera gear, studios, or professional actors by using AI avatars to deliver scripts with natural movements and synchronization. Key Capabilities AI Avatar Generation: Access a library of digital presenters or create custom avatars to deliver your message. Text-to-Speech Integration: Convert written scripts into spoken dialogue with realistic voice synthesis. Lip-Sync Technology: Advanced synchronization ensures that the avatar's mouth movements match the audio perfectly for a professional look. Rapid Production: Significantly reduce the time from scriptwriting to final video export. Best For Kai Pai is ideal for content creators, digital marketers, and corporate trainers who need to produce frequent video updates, educational tutorials, or social media ads without the overhead of a full production crew.

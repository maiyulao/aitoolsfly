## 文章看点

Airgram is an AI meeting assistant that automates transcription and distills long conversations into concise, actionable summaries across Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. It eliminates manual note-taking and saves hours of review time, allowing you to instantly track deliverables and key takeaways.

## 文章看点

Baidu AiPan integrates an intelligent AI layer directly into your Baidu Netdisk, allowing you to summarize documents and query files using natural language. It eliminates tedious manual reading and uploads by transforming your cloud storage into a searchable, automated knowledge base for instant data extraction and report generation.

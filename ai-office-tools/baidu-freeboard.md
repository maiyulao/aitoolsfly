## 文章看点

Baidu FreeBoard is an intelligent infinite canvas workspace that transforms unstructured brainstorming into professional documentation through AI-powered mind mapping and dynamic structuring. It empowers users to visualize complex ideas and seamlessly integrate cloud assets, drastically accelerating the transition from creative ideation to final reporting.

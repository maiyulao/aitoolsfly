## 文章看点

Rows is a powerful AI-driven spreadsheet that automates data cleaning and formula generation using natural language. It eliminates manual entry by integrating live data from platforms like Stripe and Google Analytics, delivering instant business intelligence through dynamic, real-time dashboards.

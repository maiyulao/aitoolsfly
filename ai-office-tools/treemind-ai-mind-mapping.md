## 文章看点

TreeMind is an AI-powered visual thinking tool that instantly transforms a single sentence into a comprehensive, structured mind map. It eliminates manual drafting and "blank page syndrome," allowing students and project managers to automate brainstorming and organize complex ideas with effortless efficiency.

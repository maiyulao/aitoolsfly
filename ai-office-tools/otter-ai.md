## 文章看点

Otter.ai automates meeting notes by converting live audio into accurate real-time transcriptions and concise summaries. It integrates seamlessly with Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams, allowing professionals and students to eliminate manual note-taking and instantly access searchable conversation archives.

## 文章看点

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant that automates recording, transcription, and the generation of actionable summaries across platforms like Zoom and Google Meet. It eliminates manual note-taking and boosts efficiency by turning conversations into a searchable knowledge base with seamless CRM integration.

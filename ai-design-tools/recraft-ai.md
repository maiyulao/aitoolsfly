## 文章看点

Recraft AI is a professional design ecosystem that transforms text into production-ready assets, including scalable vector graphics (SVG), 3D renders, and consistent style sets. It empowers designers and brand managers to rapidly prototype high-quality icons and brand identities on an infinite canvas, eliminating the tedious manual labor of traditional vector tracing.

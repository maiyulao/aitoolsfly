## 文章看点

Fabrie AI is a powerful design assistant integrated into a collaborative whiteboard, bridging the gap between brainstorming and final visual execution. It enables teams to rapidly generate and iterate on visual assets and prototypes in real-time without switching tools, significantly accelerating the mood-boarding and ideation process.

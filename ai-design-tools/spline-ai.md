## 文章看点

Spline AI transforms 3D design by allowing you to create, refine, and animate 3D assets using simple text prompts. It removes the steep learning curve of traditional CAD software, enabling UI/UX designers and developers to instantly add interactive, high-quality 3D elements to web projects.

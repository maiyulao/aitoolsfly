## 文章看点

Poly streamlines 3D asset creation by generating high-fidelity, seamless PBR textures from simple text prompts. It eliminates manual shader crafting, allowing game developers and 3D artists to instantly produce professional-grade, tileable materials for rapid prototyping and realistic rendering.

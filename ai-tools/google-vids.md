## 文章看点

Google Vids is an AI-powered video creation tool integrated into Google Workspace that transforms documents and prompts into polished business presentations. It eliminates the need for professional editing skills through automated storyboarding and asset integration, allowing teams to collaborate in real-time and produce corporate videos with maximum efficiency.

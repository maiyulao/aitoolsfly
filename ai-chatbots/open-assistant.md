## 文章看点

Open Assistant is a free, open-source conversational AI designed for natural dialogue, creative writing, and general information retrieval. It offers a transparent, community-driven alternative to proprietary chatbots, empowering developers and AI enthusiasts to explore and fine-tune LLMs without paywalls.

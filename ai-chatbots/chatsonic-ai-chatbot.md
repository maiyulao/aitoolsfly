## 文章看点

ChatSonic is a real-time AI chatbot that integrates Google Search to bypass knowledge cutoffs, delivering up-to-the-minute information and citations. It boosts productivity for creators and researchers by combining live web access with AI image generation and customizable brand personas in one intuitive interface.

## 文章看点

Inworld AI replaces static dialogue trees with dynamic, AI-driven NPCs that possess deep personalities, goals, and emotional intelligence. It empowers game developers to create immersive, unscripted real-time interactions that integrate seamlessly with Unity and Unreal Engine for an emergent storytelling experience.

## 内容预览

OverviewInworld AI transforms the way developers approach non-player characters (NPCs) by replacing static dialogue trees with dynamic, AI-driven personalities.

## 文章看点

Craft AI Assistant is an intelligence layer deeply integrated into the Craft document workspace, allowing you to generate content, rewrite text, and brainstorm ideas without leaving your canvas. It transforms raw notes into polished deliverables instantly, significantly boosting productivity for knowledge workers and creatives through a seamless, block-based editing experience.

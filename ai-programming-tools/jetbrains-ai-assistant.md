## 文章看点

JetBrains AI Assistant integrates powerful LLMs directly into your IDE, leveraging deep semantic code analysis to provide highly accurate, context-aware suggestions. It streamlines development by automating code generation, refactoring, and documentation, allowing professional engineers to boost productivity without ever leaving their editor.

OverviewJetBrains AI Assistant is a deeply integrated AI developer tool that brings the power of large language models directly into the IDEs you already use.

## 文章看点

Boxy is a native AI coding assistant deeply integrated into the CodeSandbox ecosystem, providing real-time, context-aware code generation and instant debugging. It eliminates local setup and accelerates prototyping by automating repetitive tasks directly within your cloud development environment.

## 内容预览

OverviewBoxy is the native AI coding assistant integrated into the CodeSandbox ecosystem. Unlike standalone AI plugins, Boxy is deeply embedded within the cloud development environment, allowing it to understand the context of your project, dependencies, and runtime errors in real-time.Key CapabilitiesContext-Aware Code Generation: Generate boilerplate, functions, or complex logic based on your existing project structure.Integrated Debugging: Quickly identify the root cause of errors and apply AI-suggested fixes without leaving the editor.Rapid Prototyping: Accelerate the transition from idea to deployment by automating repetitive coding tasks.Seamless Cloud Integration: Works natively within CodeSandbox, eliminating the need for local environment configuration.Best ForBoxy is ideal for frontend and full-stack developers who utilize CodeSandbox for rapid prototyping, collaborative coding, or hosting demo projects. It is particularly useful for those who want an AI assistant that understands the specific nuances of a cloud-based development workflow.Limitations and PricingAs Boxy is integrated into CodeSandbox, its availability and usage limits are tied to your CodeSandbox account tier.

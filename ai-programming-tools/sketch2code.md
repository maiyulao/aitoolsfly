## 内容预览

Sketch2Code is an innovative experimental tool developed by Microsoft AI Lab designed to bridge the gap between conceptual design and initial development. The generated HTML may require significant manual refinement to meet modern accessibility standards, responsive design requirements, and specific brand styling.

## 文章看点

HeyCLI transforms natural language descriptions into accurate shell commands, eliminating the need to memorize obscure flags or search through documentation. It significantly boosts terminal productivity and reduces errors, allowing developers to execute complex tasks instantly without switching between the browser and the console.

## 文章看点

Nexu is a free, open-source desktop client for OpenClaw that replaces complex command-line setups with a streamlined graphical interface. It allows users to effortlessly manage AI agents and monitor activities in real-time, providing a lightweight and accessible way to orchestrate AI workflows locally.

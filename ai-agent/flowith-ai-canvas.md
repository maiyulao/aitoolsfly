## 文章看点

Flowith replaces linear chat with a visual canvas, allowing you to map out complex workflows and branch ideas into node-based paths. It eliminates context drift by integrating multiple LLMs into a spatial layout, significantly boosting efficiency for research, coding, and large-scale project organization.

## 内容预览

A Visual Approach to AI InteractionFlowith departs from the traditional linear chat interface, offering a canvas-based environment where users can map out their thoughts and AI interactions visually. Each prompt becomes a node, allowing you to branch into different directions without losing the context of your original query.Iterative Refinement: Easily pivot or refine specific parts of a workflow by modifying individual nodes, making it ideal for research, coding, and content strategy.Spatial Organization: Manage large-scale projects by grouping related AI interactions, preventing the 'context drift' often found in long chat threads.Best ForFlowith is particularly effective for power users who find standard chatbots limiting.

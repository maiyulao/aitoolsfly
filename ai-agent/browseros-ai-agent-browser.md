## 文章看点

BrowserOS is an open-source AI-native browser that transforms passive browsing into an automated experience by delegating complex, multi-step web tasks to autonomous agents. It empowers users to instantly eliminate repetitive research and data extraction through seamless DOM-level automation for maximum productivity.

## 内容预览

BrowserOS is an innovative, open-source web browser engineered specifically for the era of AI agents.

## 文章看点

Udio is a powerful generative AI that transforms text prompts into high-fidelity, full-length songs with realistic vocals and instrumentation across any genre. It empowers creators and hobbyists to instantly produce studio-quality music and royalty-free soundtracks without any formal musical training.

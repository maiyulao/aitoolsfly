## 文章看点

AssemblyAI provides developers with high-accuracy speech-to-text APIs and audio intelligence tools to convert spoken language into actionable data. It streamlines the creation of call analytics and automated meeting notes through powerful features like speaker diarization, sentiment analysis, and PII redaction.

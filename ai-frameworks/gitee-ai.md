## 文章看点

Gitee AI is a collaborative co-creation ecosystem that streamlines the entire lifecycle of AI applications from coding to production. It eliminates infrastructure complexity, allowing teams to rapidly develop, iterate, and deploy AI tools within a unified workspace for faster time-to-market.

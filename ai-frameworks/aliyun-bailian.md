## 文章看点

Aliyun Bailian is a professional platform for developing and orchestrating AI applications, bridging the gap between raw LLMs and production-ready solutions. It empowers enterprises to rapidly build custom AI agents through low-code orchestration, RAG pipelines, and simplified fine-tuning tools without managing complex GPU infrastructure.

## 内容预览

Aliyun Bailian is a professional AI model development and application orchestration platform designed to bridge the gap between raw large language models (LLMs) and production-ready business applications. Users should be aware that deep integration with the Alibaba Cloud ecosystem may lead to platform lock-in.

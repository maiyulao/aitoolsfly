## 文章看点

Refly is an open-source platform that lets you build fluid, non-linear "Vibe Workflows" for AI applications, breaking the constraints of rigid traditional pipelines. It enables developers to rapidly prototype and deploy adaptive AI agents with full customization and seamless LLM integration.

## 内容预览

Refly is an innovative open-source platform introducing the concept of 'Vibe Workflows' to the AI development ecosystem. It aims to bridge the gap between complex backend AI orchestration and intuitive user experiences, allowing developers to build fluid, adaptive AI applications without the rigid constraints of traditional linear pipelines.Key CapabilitiesOpen-Source Framework: Provides a transparent and customizable foundation for developers to build their own AI orchestration layers.Vibe Workflow Orchestration: Enables the creation of non-linear, dynamic workflows that can adapt based on the context and output of AI models.Rapid Prototyping: Streamlines the transition from an initial AI concept to a functional deployment through its specialized development environment.Extensible Integration: Designed to work with various LLMs and external APIs to create comprehensive AI agents.Best ForRefly is ideal for AI engineers, independent developers, and enterprise teams who are building complex AI agents or multi-step automation processes and require an open-source alternative to proprietary orchestration tools.Limitations and PricingAs an open-source project, the primary cost is the infrastructure required for self-hosting.

## 文章看点

Gumloop is a no-code AI automation platform that lets you build complex data pipelines and autonomous agents using a visual drag-and-drop canvas. It empowers non-engineers to instantly automate data-heavy tasks like lead generation and research, eliminating the need for a full development team.

## 文章看点

Infinigence is an enterprise-grade AI infrastructure platform that simplifies the entire lifecycle of large-scale model development, from training to deployment. It eliminates hardware management complexities by providing unified computing power and streamlined orchestration, allowing teams to deploy proprietary AI applications with maximum efficiency and scalability.

## 内容预览

OverviewPerplexity AI reimagines the traditional search engine experience by shifting from a list of links to a direct, conversational answer engine. Instead of browsing multiple websites to synthesize information, users receive a comprehensive response backed by real-time web data and verifiable citations.Key CapabilitiesReal-Time Web Indexing: Unlike static LLMs, Perplexity scans the live web to provide the most current information available.Transparent Citations: Every claim is supported by inline citations, allowing users to verify facts and explore original sources instantly.Pro Discovery Mode: An advanced research mode that asks clarifying questions to narrow down the user's intent for more precise results.Multi-Model Integration: Depending on the plan, users can toggle between different state-of-the-art AI models to refine the quality of the output.Best ForPerplexity is ideal for students, researchers, journalists, and professionals who require factual accuracy and source attribution. It is particularly effective for deep-dive research, competitive analysis, and staying updated on rapidly evolving news topics.Limitations & PricingWhile the basic search functionality is free, advanced features—such as increased file uploads and access to premium models (e.g., GPT-4o or Claude 3)—require a monthly subscription. Users should be aware that while citations reduce hallucinations, the AI can still occasionally misinterpret complex source material.Disclaimer: Features and pricing are subject to change.

## 文章看点

Tiangong AI Search transforms traditional searching into a generative experience, delivering synthesized, real-time answers with clear source citations instead of simple links. It empowers researchers and professionals to instantly synthesize complex information and eliminate manual filtering for maximum efficiency.

## 内容预览

OverviewTiangong AI Search represents a shift from traditional keyword-based indexing to a semantic, generative approach to information retrieval. Developed by Kunlun Tech, the platform integrates a sophisticated Large Language Model (LLM) with real-time web crawling to deliver comprehensive answers instead of just a list of links.Key CapabilitiesGenerative Answers: Instead of browsing multiple pages, users receive a synthesized summary that directly addresses their query.Real-time Web Integration: The engine continuously indexes the web to ensure that responses include current events and up-to-date data.Source Attribution: To ensure transparency and combat hallucinations, the tool provides citations and links to the original sources used to generate the answer.Complex Query Handling: Capable of processing nuanced, long-form questions that traditional search engines often struggle to parse.Best ForTiangong AI Search is ideal for researchers, students, and professionals who need to synthesize large amounts of information quickly or find specific answers within a vast sea of web data without manual filtering.Limitations and PricingAs an evolving AI product, users may encounter occasional inaccuracies typical of LLMs. While basic search functionality is currently accessible, specific advanced features or API access may be subject to future pricing tiers.

## 文章看点

iAsk AI is a next-generation search engine that synthesizes real-time web data into direct, comprehensive answers, eliminating the need to click through multiple links. It boosts research efficiency by providing verified citations and coherent summaries, allowing users to gather factual information and track sources instantly.

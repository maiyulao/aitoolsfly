## 文章看点

SearchGPT transforms web searching into a conversational experience, providing direct, real-time answers with inline citations instead of lists of links. It eliminates the need to sift through SEO pages, offering a high-efficiency way to conduct complex research and track live events instantly.

## 内容预览

OverviewSearchGPT is a specialized AI-powered search experience developed by OpenAI. Unlike traditional search engines that provide a list of links, SearchGPT synthesizes real-time information from the web to provide direct, conversational answers. It aims to bridge the gap between the vast knowledge of a Large Language Model (LLM) and the current, live data of the internet.Key CapabilitiesReal-Time Web Access: Continuously crawls and indexes the web to provide up-to-date information on current events, weather, and news.Inline Citations: Every claim is backed by clear sources, allowing users to verify information and dive deeper into original publisher content.Conversational Context: Users can ask follow-up questions, and the AI remembers the context of the search session for a more natural discovery process.Publisher Integration: Designed to work with publishers to ensure a fair ecosystem where traffic is directed back to the original content creators.Best ForSearchGPT is ideal for users who want the speed of a search engine combined with the reasoning capabilities of a chatbot. It is particularly useful for complex research tasks, tracking live events, and finding specific information without sifting through pages of SEO-optimized blue links.Limitations and PricingAs SearchGPT has been introduced as a prototype/preview, availability may be limited to specific regions or waitlisted users.

## 文章看点

Glean is an AI-powered enterprise search platform that centralizes company knowledge across tools like Slack, Google Drive, and Jira into a single search bar. It eliminates manual searching by providing direct, generative AI answers while respecting strict internal permission controls to ensure data security.

## 内容预览

Centralize Your Company KnowledgeGlean is an advanced AI-powered search and knowledge management platform designed specifically for the enterprise. Instead of manually searching through dozens of different apps, Glean provides a single, unified search bar that indexes your entire company's ecosystem—including Slack, Google Drive, Jira, GitHub, and Microsoft 365.Key CapabilitiesUnified Enterprise Search: Find documents, conversations, and project updates across all fragmented SaaS tools from one interface.AI-Powered Answers: Beyond simple links, Glean uses generative AI to synthesize information and provide direct answers to complex internal questions.Permission-Aware Indexing: The system respects existing access controls, ensuring users only see information they are authorized to view.Knowledge Discovery: Automatically identifies subject matter experts within your organization based on their activity and documentation.Best ForGlean is ideal for mid-to-large scale organizations with complex data landscapes where employees spend significant time searching for internal information or onboarding new team members.Limitations and PricingGlean is an enterprise-grade solution and typically requires a custom quote based on seat count and integration needs.

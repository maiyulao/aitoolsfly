## 文章看点

Rubriq is a specialized AI writing assistant designed to polish academic manuscripts and provide high-fidelity technical translations for researchers. It helps PhD students and professors elevate their writing to publication quality by ensuring formal tone, linguistic flow, and precision in scholarly communication.
